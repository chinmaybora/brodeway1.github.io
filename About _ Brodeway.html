About Us

LET’S GROW YOUR BRAND ON SOCIAL MEDIA

Welcome to the family!

Established in 2017, Brodeway is a multi-channel network
and digital marketing company that endeavours to promote
organizations and self-employed people

We embarked on our marketing journey with Vigo video and
our valuable clients include Godrej, Roposo, Soul Flower,
Likee, Bytedance, and Kwai India. So far we’ve worked with
2k+social media influencers to promote organizations and
individuals.

What We Do

protection against business factors you can’t
control”

SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Our experts facilitate the management of your social

PUBLIC RELATIONS SOLUTIONS

Our PR experts develop media relation strategies, handle marketing communications, produce impactful content for press releases and regularly
analyze market trends to strengthen your presence in the market

INTERIOR AND ARCHITECTURE CONSULTANCY

EVENT MANAGEMENT

May it be seminars, conferences or brand launch events, our team strives to make every event a grand success.
